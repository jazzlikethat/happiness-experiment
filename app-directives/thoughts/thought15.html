Meditation

Stressed – try meditation.

Overwhelmed – try meditation.

It is easy for us to get sucked into the busyness of our day. Checking our phones at every ding. Responding within seconds so that our friends continue to like us.

Meditation is an ancient practice that allows us to escape from the noise of the day and train our thoughts on self awareness.

According to Headspace (a leading meditation app) Meditation is: “Meditation isn’t about becoming a different person, a new person, or even a better person. It’s about training in awareness and getting a healthy sense of perspective. You’re not trying to turn off your thoughts or feelings. You’re learning to observe them without judgment. And eventually, you may start to better understand them as well.” The Wikipedia page on meditation is great place to start your investigation into yoga.

At its core, Meditation is about training our attention and awareness. With practice meditation helps us become more relaxed, calm, clear and alert. Meditation is used to create space in our minds and foster general mental well-being.

Meditation is designed to create post mediation mindfulness, space, energy and focus.

The most important question in meditation is - How are you relating to what is happening?
