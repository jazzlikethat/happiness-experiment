Reflection

We do hundreds of things every day, but they just all seems to blend together. We lose track of all the good things that have happened and all the good things that are in our life, instead we focus on just one or two negative experiences or frustrations.

Take time at the end of each day to reflect on the good that has happened in your day, write or express gratitude and appreciation for those good things, good people good things in your life.
