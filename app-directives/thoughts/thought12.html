Exercise

Perhaps you have heard the expression that "The physical drives the mental."

There is little question that the quality of your life has a lot to do with your physical condition and your beliefs about your condition.

We believe in a blend of exercises that encompass: Strength, flexibility, agility, speed, and endurance.

Life works against you. Many of our jobs require us to sit for extended periods hunched over a keyboard.

In our relaxation time we lounge on the couch watching TV. Speaking of TV here is a scary statistic, in a recent survey it was found that the average American watches the equivalent of 16 years of TV in their lifetime.

Walk every day. 10,000 steps +

Strength training – minimum of 3 times per week.

Flexibility training – minimum of 3 times per week and then daily during 20 minute work bursts.

Endurance training – minimum of 3 times per week.

Speed training – minimum of 3 times per week.

Please note that no times are given each workout. The duration is up to you and must be worked into your daily balance. Trainings can be combined into a single session.

Tip:Find and follow an expert online or in your local gym. Some of our favorites include Paul Chek, Coach Sommer (Gymnastic strength training).

Action:Start your exercise routine today. Block out your exercise time in your daily calendar for tomorrow and then get started. No excuses. Record your results in your spiral notebook.
