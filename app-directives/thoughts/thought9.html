Affirmation

So your thinking – Affirmation – What is an affirmation?.

An affirmation is a positive statement that you say to yourself over and over. An affirmation trains your mind. In training your mind it influences your future.

I am… (strong, courageous, caring, disciplined, happy, etc.)

I can… (control my day, reach my goal, be a better friend, etc.)

I will… (always give it my best effort, reach my sales goals, etc.)

This is just another way to load up your day with positivity. It has been proven that Positive Affect has a significant impact on your overall happiness.

"How you think does determine who you’ll become." Zig Ziglar

"Life is an echo. What you send out comes back. What you sow, you reap." Zig Ziglar.

Tip:Write down 3 affirmations on sticky notes and put them up on your bedroom or bathroom mirror.

Action:Practice your affirmations saying them out loud as your look at yourself in the mirror. Experiment with different affirmations. Write your observations in your spiral notebook.

Note: this is going to feel ridiculous at first, power through, start short and progressively add time each day. We recommend that you strive for a minimum of 2 minutes of genuine affirmation as part of your morning routine. As an added bonus add affirmations to your bedtime routine as well.
