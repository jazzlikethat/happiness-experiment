Sleep

Sleep is one of the most overlooked foundations of happiness. Think of the last time you were truly rested. How did you feel? Energetic, Alive? Now think about the last time you were sleep deprived. How did you feel? If your like me you felt sluggish, lethargic, easily agitated.

Science tells us that we need between 7-9 hours of sleep per night. How are you setting yourself up for success?

Do you keep your phone by your bed?

Do you keep your room dark?

Are you using electronics before bed?

Do you check your phone during the night?

Arianna Huffington’s company Thrive Global has created an amazing resource on their website filled with articles and helpful tips.

Start tonight. Turn off your electronics an hour before bed. Eliminate all your electronics from your bedroom. Read a good book before bed. Use an alarm clock to wake yourself up. It is helpful if you go to bed and wake up at the same time everyday to get into circadian rhythms and daily flow. For me I make it a habit to be in bed by 930 to that I can wake up rested at 5am.

​

Tip:Practice today. Make it fun. Reward yourself after you finish. You will get better over time.
