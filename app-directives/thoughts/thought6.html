The Happiest People That I Know

The Happiest People that I know:

  • Choose to be happy. Positive. Optimistic.
  • Have great families – multi generational
  • Their spouse or significant other is their life partner
  • Have close friends and can talk about anything
  • Are themselves (no masks). Authentic.
  • Genuinely care about others – good listeners, provide candid feedback
  • Volunteer in the community
  • Are good at what they do and love doing it.
  • Have purpose and make an impact.
  • Continuously learn and evolve.
  • Have money under control, frugal, debt free
  • Prefer experiences over material things.
  • Understand that they control much of every experience.
  • Are healthy, enjoy exercise and nature.
  • Live (savor) Life – Enjoy food, drink and music.
  • Live in the moment.
  • They sing, dance, laugh and, in general, “Love Life.”
  • Have a daily routine and start early.
  • Struggle Well. Communicate openly and honestly no matter the topic.
  • Are humble and have high expectations.

Action: Write down (in your spiral notebook) a list of the 5 happiest people you know and why you think they are so happy.
