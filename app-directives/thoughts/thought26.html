Fear Setting

“Show me your friends and I will show you your future.” Anonymous

“We are the average of the 5 people that we spend the most time with.” Jim Rohn

Think about this for a minute. Now think about the 5 people you spend the most time with.

What do you want out of life?

Are these 5 people helping you get there? Do they lift you up or drag you down?

I challenge you to look deep go beyond the superficial exterior.

Think about the 5 people you would like to spend the most time with if it could be anybody on the planet. Why did you choose these 5 people.

This may be the most important thought exercise of the 29 why? Meaningful relationships and personal growth are 2 of the most important factors in living a good life.

Action:Write down the 5 people that you spend the most time with. Then write down the 5 people that you would like to spend the most time with and why. Write your observations down in your spiral notebook.
